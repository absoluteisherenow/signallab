Direct Outreach

Highest-conversion channel. Sold out NDR 2 on personal messages alone. Three waves, escalating urgency, never bluffing.

Wave 1: Inner Circle
Apr 21-25
Close friends, regular gig-goers, people who'd come regardless. 30-50 messages. Personal, not templated. After they commit, ask them to share.
Target: 15-25 confirmed tickets
Wave 2: Extended Network
May 5-9
Industry contacts, DJs, producers, label people, venue staff, scene regulars. 50-80 messages. Wave 1 social proof gives you credibility.
Target: 20-30 tickets (many bring +1s)
Wave 3: Final Push
Jun 2-6
Anyone from Wave 1+2 who didn't respond + wider London contacts. 30-50 messages. Only send if tickets are genuinely selling. Never bluff.
Target: 10-20 tickets

Ad Management
Daily Protocol
48-Hour Rule
Non-negotiable
Rule Any ad with CTR below 1% after 48hrs: kill it
Frequency Any ad set over 3.0 frequency: pause it
CPC ceiling Above £0.50 for 3+ days: kill it
Creative Rotation
Every 7-10 days
Rule Never run same creative longer than 10 days
Prep Next week's creative ready before killing current
Negative comments Kill immediately. Credibility damage.
Scale Triggers
When to push harder
CTR above 2% Increase daily budget by 20%
Engagement above 5% Expand to lookalike audiences
Organic post 2x avg Boost same day via Campaign 4
Creative Rules
Always
Source All creative = NM's organic content. Never stock.
Aesthetic Dark/moody matching the feed. Seamless click-through.
Tone No "BUY TICKETS" energy. Lead with the art.
