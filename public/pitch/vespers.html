Strategy: Engagement first, conversion second. We build a warm audience from content before asking anyone to buy a ticket. Retargeting closes the gap. All creative uses NM's organic content and visual language.

Targeting: London 25km, ages 21-44. Interest stacking across Resident Advisor, Boiler Room, Bicep, Four Tet, Jamie xx, Floating Points, Disclosure, Cercle. Layered with 1% lookalike audiences built from our existing engagers. Existing followers excluded from all prospecting.

Management: Daily performance monitoring. Creative rotated every 7-10 days to prevent fatigue. Any ad below 1% CTR killed within 48 hours. Budget shifted in real-time toward best-performing creative and audiences. Full retargeting funnel from first engagement through to ticket purchase.

Personal outreach: Direct WhatsApp campaign to our London network alongside the paid media. Proven channel. We sold out a comparable room on personal outreach alone.
